# Final Approved English Course Content

This document contains the final approved English content for the six programs at Edu Creativity Center.

## 1. General English Program
- **SEO title**: General English Program | Edu Creativity Center
- **Meta description**: Develop a solid foundation across all core language skills with our online General English Program.
- **H1**: General English Program
- **Short course-card description**: A comprehensive online course balancing grammar, vocabulary, reading, and listening for everyday communication.
- **Hero introduction**: The General English Program helps learners develop a solid foundation across all core language skills. Through structured, interactive online sessions, you will build the confidence needed to communicate clearly in everyday situations.
- **Who the program supports**: Learners looking for a balanced, step-by-step approach to improving their overall English proficiency.
- **Main learning goals**: Overcoming hesitation when speaking, expanding vocabulary for daily use, and understanding essential grammar in context.
- **Skills covered**: Speaking, listening, reading, writing, practical grammar, and daily vocabulary.
- **Delivery format**: Live online delivery ensuring real-time interaction, immediate feedback, and guided practice.
- **Group or individual format**: Group format.
- **Recordings and review-material wording**: Selected lesson recordings, exercises, and review materials are shared with enrolled students without a fixed expiry, subject to continued storage and service availability.
- **What learners receive**: Live group lessons, guided exercises, review materials, and instructor feedback during learning activities.
- **Registration process**: Register online, review your level and goals with the academy, and receive guidance on the right starting point.
- **Course-fees contact wording**: Contact us for current course fees and available schedules.
- **FAQs**: 
  - *Do I need prior experience?* We review your current level upon registration to place you correctly.
  - *Are classes live?* Yes, all lessons are led by an instructor live online.
- **Final call to action**: Register Online or Contact us for current course fees and available schedules via WhatsApp.
- **Internal-link recommendations**: Link to English Conversation Program.
- **Structured-data recommendation**: `Course`
- **Content exclusions**: No adult-only wording, no professional-result implications, no fluency guarantees.
- **Remaining curriculum details that must not yet be published**: Exact duration, schedule, lesson count, trial-lesson availability, exact CEFR range, teacher names.
