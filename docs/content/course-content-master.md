# Course Content Master

This document contains the official original English content blueprints for the six approved programs at Edu Creativity Center. All wording is fully original, accurate to the online delivery model, and free of unsupported marketing claims.

## 1. General English Program

- **Official English name**: General English Program
- **Short card description**: A comprehensive online course balancing grammar, vocabulary, reading, and listening for everyday communication.
- **Full page introduction**: The General English Program helps learners develop a solid foundation across all core language skills. Through structured, interactive online sessions, you will build the confidence needed to communicate clearly in everyday situations.
- **Who the program is for**: Learners looking for a balanced, step-by-step approach to improving their overall English proficiency.
- **Main learning needs addressed**: Overcoming hesitation when speaking, expanding vocabulary for daily use, and understanding essential grammar in context.
- **Skills covered**: Speaking, listening, reading, writing, practical grammar, and daily vocabulary.
- **Live online delivery explanation**: Lessons are conducted live online with an instructor, ensuring real-time interaction, immediate feedback, and guided practice.
- **Group or individual format**: Group-based program.
- **Recordings and review-material wording**: Selected lesson recordings, exercises, and review materials are shared with enrolled students without a fixed expiry, subject to continued storage and service availability.
- **What the learner receives**: Live group lessons, guided exercises, review materials, and instructor feedback during learning activities.
- **How the learner starts**: Register online, review your level and goals with the academy, and receive guidance on the right starting point in the General English Program.
- **Realistic learning objectives**: Improve sentence structure, handle common social interactions with confidence, and comprehend standard spoken English.
- **What the program does not promise**: Does not guarantee immediate fluency or official CEFR certification.
- **Suggested calls to action**: "Contact us for current course fees" and "Register Online".
- **Frequently asked questions**:
  - *Do I need prior experience?* We review your current level upon registration to place you correctly.
  - *Are classes live?* Yes, all lessons are led by an instructor live online.
- **Internal-link recommendations**: Link to Contact Us, English Conversation Program.
- **Recommended structured-data type**: `Course`
- **Missing curriculum details**: Exact duration, schedule, and lesson count (pending owner approval).
